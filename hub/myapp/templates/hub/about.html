About Erasmus+ Hub

Connecting individuals to international Erasmus+ opportunities and fostering meaningful global collaborations.

Our Mission

Erasmus+ Hub is your go-to platform for discovering and applying for Erasmus+ projects worldwide. Our mission is to empower individuals with access to global educational and cultural experiences.

Our Journey

2022 - Idea Born

The idea of Erasmus+ Hub was conceptualized to simplify access to international projects.

2023 - First Projects Launched

The platform started listing authentic Erasmus+ projects, connecting applicants worldwide.

2024 - Community Growth

Over 10,000 participants successfully applied for Erasmus+ projects through our platform.
